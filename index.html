Bio

DjeDje Music is a unique record label//production company aimed at recognizing artists who exemplify cultural diversity and dedidation. Featuring Haitian Kompa and HipHop, Island Reggea and Dance Hall, DjeDje Music represents the best the carribean has to offer.

Run by Haitian singer/songwritter Wood Jerry Remelus (aka Dr Wood aka DjeDje). This company provides opportunity for lesser known artists to produce and perform their cultural sounds and creations.
